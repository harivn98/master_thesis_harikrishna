B.10. Quality Classification Thresholds
================================================================================

The three-level quality classification applied to each generated video, as implemented in VideoPainter's evaluation module:

    Level        Criteria (at least N of the following)       N
    ----------   ----------------------------------------   ---
    EXCELLENT    PSNR > 40 dB, SSIM > 0.95, LPIPS < 0.1     2
    GOOD         PSNR > 40 dB, SSIM > 0.95, LPIPS < 0.1     1
    MODERATE     None of the above thresholds met             0

    Run 006 result: All 1,000 base videos and all 100 LoRA videos received
    MODERATE classification. This is expected because the inpainting replaces
    the entire road surface, producing large pixel-level differences from the
    original (PSNR ~11 dB, SSIM ~0.51, LPIPS ~0.46). The MODERATE
    classification reflects the magnitude of the edit, not the quality of the
    generated content.
